\section*{The case study (in plain English)}

\textbf{[SCAFFOLD --- AW: ~300 words. Tell the story of the 27-hour
on-chain intervention as a story, not a results dump. Anchors:
\begin{itemize}
\item Day 1, early morning UTC: token dropped ~60\% on a single
      Solana pool.
\item The agentic policy was activated under a constitutional gate
      (paused-by-default, board-sign-off-required). 52 small buys
      followed over 27 hours.
\item Two stop-loss events tripped the safety circuit; both were
      manually overridden after explicit board sign-off.
\item Outcome: price recovered ~20\% from the floor; pool liquidity
      grew from \$5.4k to \$10.5k.
\item One organic buyer arrived at the new defended floor without
      the policy firing.
\end{itemize}
Tone: calm, factual, reflective. The point is the architecture worked
under the constitutional constraints, not that the price went up.]}

\textbf{[SCAFFOLD --- AW: ~120 words. Honest disclaimer paragraph. Three
